FAQs

Common Passport Questions

What is the difference between e-filling.in and the official Passport Seva portal?▾
e-filling.in is a private facilitation service. We assist you in correctly filling the passport form, preparing documents, and booking your PSK appointment on the official passportindia.gov.in portal. All government fees go directly to the Government of India. We charge a separate professional service fee for our assistance only. We are not affiliated with the government.
How long does it take to get a passport?▾
Under Normal scheme: 30–45 working days after the PSK visit. Under Tatkal scheme: 7–14 working days. Timing depends on police verification status and the regional passport office workload — both controlled solely by the Government of India.
Is Aadhaar mandatory for a passport application?▾
Aadhaar is the most accepted document for address and identity proof and significantly speeds up the process. Voter ID, bank passbook, or utility bills are accepted alternatives, but Aadhaar is strongly recommended.
Do you serve applicants from all states in India?▾
Yes, our online facilitation is available for applicants across all states and union territories. You do not need to visit our New Delhi office. Everything is handled via phone, email, or WhatsApp.

FAQs

Common GST Questions

Who needs to register for GST?▾
Any business with annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakh (₹10 lakh for special category states) must register under GST. Businesses supplying goods interstate, e-commerce sellers, or those providing taxable services must register regardless of turnover.
What is the penalty for late GST return filing?▾
Late filing attracts ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for returns with tax liability, and ₹20 per day for nil returns, plus 18% per annum interest on outstanding tax. Filing on time with our help avoids these costs entirely.
Is GST registration free on the government portal?▾
Yes, GST registration on gst.gov.in is free of government charges. Our service fee is for professional assistance — form filling, document preparation, and submission — to ensure accuracy and avoid rejection due to errors.
How long does GST registration take?▾
If documents are complete and correct, GST registration is typically approved within 3–7 working days by the GST authorities. Incomplete applications may be queried, causing delays — which our expert team helps you avoid.
